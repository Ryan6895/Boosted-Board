WHY A BELT DRIVE SYSTEM?

Our belt­ drive system magnifies the torque* of the motor by 3x, giving you stronger brakes, faster acceleration, and steeper hill climbing than hub motors. Belts are also quiet and more precise, giving you a better ride with more control. *Fancy physics term for how much force is needed to cause an object to rotate.

Bamboo Deck by Loaded

We collaborated with Loaded to create a custom-designed Vanguard deck. The bamboo provides natural suspension and damping over bumps, while still delivering enough energy for a snappy, responsive ride. The flex and feel of this board cannot be replicated by decks made from a bamboo veneer slapped over a maple core. Try it-you'll feel the difference.

80mm Orangatang Kegel Wheels

The Orangatang wheels have a carefully designed hourglass shape and urethane formula that flexes over bumps and grips through turns. By coupling the drivetrain to the wheel's existing core, we maintain the wheel's amazing ride quality and handling better than any other hub motor design.
